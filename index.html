Core Insight

Winning Game 2 matters, but why it matters depends on the series state.

The overall Game 2 winner win rate was 73.94%. However, when the Game 2 winner also won Game 1 and took a 2-0 lead, the series win rate rose to 89.12%. When the Game 2 winner only tied the series 1-1, the win rate was 52.55%.
