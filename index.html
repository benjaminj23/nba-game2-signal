Limitations

What this model does not know

This analysis shows association, not causation. The model does not fully control for injuries, player availability, team strength, roster changes, coaching adjustments, or matchup-specific factors. Later-round samples are smaller, and 2026 validation is based only on completed series available at the time of testing.
